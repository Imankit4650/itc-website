[ 02 ]  About ITC

Mission, Vision
& Purpose

ITC coordinates every club, event, and technical initiative at IIT Bombay — shaping the institute's identity as a hub of innovation.

01

Our Mission

To foster a culture of technical excellence, collaboration, and innovation — giving every student the resources and mentorship to turn curiosity into creation.

02

Our Vision

A campus where every student is an innovator. IIT Bombay as the breeding ground for the next generation of engineers, researchers, and tech entrepreneurs.

03

What We Do

ITC oversees 15+ technical clubs spanning robotics, software, electronics, astronomy, and more. We organize Techfest — Asia's largest science and tech festival — along with competitions, workshops, and speaker series year-round.

Techfest Inter IIT Tech XLR8 Hackathons Workshops Project Grants Speaker Talks
04

Student Impact

Managing IIT Bombay's technical budget, allocating resources to member clubs, and ensuring every student has a platform to pursue technical excellence.

05

Industry Interface

Bridging students with top companies, startups, and research institutions for internships, collaborations, and real-world problem-solving.
